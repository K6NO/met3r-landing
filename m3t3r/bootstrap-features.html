Features

Track consumption in real-time

With M;T3R you can track your current consumption in real-time and have access to historical data with the tap of your finger.

Achieve maximum savings

Personalised saving tips, real-time feedback on your actions and predictive analysis of your reduced consumption allows you to find the best way to achieve savings.

Set targets or compare yourself with similar households

Set your weekly or monthly energy-saving targets and see your progress in real-time. Compare your home with similar households’ consumption.

App or device?

The M;T3R app in itself allows for logging and interpreting your consumption on an ad-hoc basis. If you decide to install the inexpensive and non-invasive M;T3R device, a simple, but powerful smart metering tool, then data recording is continuous, real-time and granule to identify individual appliances.

Easy installation

Installing MET3R will be safe, quick and easy. Anyone can easily install the MET3R device in 5 minutes on the meter box, therefore MET3R’s wider user base is not limited to home-owners but expands to tenants motivated to save costs, and/or reduce their environmental footprint.

Top class privacy

With M;T3R you get 21st century privacy-protection. Your data is encrypted end-to-end and stored in a blockchain-based database. Using smart home devices results in a huge amount of sensitive personal data. This data belongs to you and with M;T3R only you are empowered to access and share it with others.

Enjoy security & peace of mind

M;T3R sends you real-time alerts on your phone if an appliance has broken down, left on unintentionally (kettle), or if there is unusual activity in your home while you are away (burglary or energy theft).

Become a Climate Hero

M;T3R helps you to minimise your carbon footprint. You can achieve maximum savings by following MET3R's personalised tips and identifying which appliance is wasteful in your household and should be upgraded. Families that monitor home energy use can reduce their consumption and carbon footprint by 15%.

Streamline relations with your utility

With M;T3R you can verify your bills, identify cheaper tariffs or providers and avoid paying more than necessary. If you are on flat rates or a prepaid customer you can set up notificatiosn to stay within your limits. You can also easily report periodic meter readings to your provider with a single click.
